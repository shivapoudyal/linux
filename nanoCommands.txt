1) Select a specific line or word -> Alt + a
2) Copy selected line or word -> Alt + ^
3) Cut a line or word -> Alt + k
4) Paste a cutted or copied line -> Cntrl + u
5) Go to bottom of the page -> Alt + /
6) Go to top of the page -> Alt + \
7) Go to line no -> Alt + g
8) Searching a word -> Cntrl + w
9) Make Undo -> Esc + u
10) Make Redo -> Esc + e
